About Oreo

The Oreo company has been making it's world famous creme-filled cookies for over 100 years! Founded in 1912, the Oreo company has expanded to over 100 countries worldwide! We have made it our mission to empower people to snack right, and this mission has helped us become one of the biggest cookie manufacturers in the world!
